What ShowingDay Does

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Six tools that used to live in six different apps, now in one focused workflow.

🗺
Smart Route Optimization

Enter your properties and get an optimized driving order with real travel times from Google Maps. No more guessing at how long the day will take.

📅
One-Click Calendar Blocks

Tentative showing blocks and travel buffers added to Google Calendar automatically. Confirmed = updated. Declined = removed. No manual editing.

⚡
Real-Time Status Updates

Connect ShowingTime via webhook and watch confirmation statuses update automatically — no manual toggling, no tab-switching, no refreshing.

🔍
Property Research on Confirm

The moment a showing is confirmed, get listing data, price history, days on market, and an AI-powered disclosure red-flag analysis.

📱
Client-Facing Itinerary

Generate a clean, shareable showing day page for your client with addresses, times, property photos, and a one-click calendar invite.

🏆
Built for West Michigan

Designed for the Allegan County / Grand Rapids market. Properties spaced 15–30 minutes apart? Rural routes with no reliable transit? This was built for you.

The Workflow

Three steps. Zero spreadsheets.

From client name to client itinerary in one focused session.

1
Enter your client & properties

Search your CRM (Lofty or GHL) or type a name. Paste property addresses, set your time window, choose start-loaded or end-loaded scheduling.

2
Optimize & schedule

Google Maps calculates the fastest route with real-time traffic. You get a complete showing day schedule — every stop, every time, every drive — in seconds.

3
Request, confirm & deliver

Submit ShowingTime requests from the built-in checklist. Track confirmations live. Then send your client a beautiful itinerary with one click.
