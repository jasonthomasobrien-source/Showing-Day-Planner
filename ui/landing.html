The Problem
Showing day planning is a scattered mess.
You're juggling Google Maps, ShowingTime, your calendar, your CRM, and a notepad — all at once. Drive times are guesses. Calendar blocks are manual. The client gets a text with addresses in no particular order.

One declined showing throws off the whole day. There's no system. There's just you, stressed, on a Saturday morning.
The Solution
AI-optimized showing day. In minutes. Not hours.
Enter a client name and your property addresses. ShowingDay pulls contact details from your CRM, calculates the optimal driving route with live traffic, blocks your calendar, generates ShowingTime request checklists, and builds a client-facing itinerary page — all from one screen.

When a showing is confirmed, property research runs automatically. When one is declined, the schedule auto-compresses.

How It Works

From client name to confirmed
route in minutes

Eight steps, automated where possible. You stay in control — the app handles the heavy lifting.

1
Enter client & addresses
Search by name — contact details auto-pulled from Lofty or GHL. Add property addresses one by one or paste a list.
⚡ Auto CRM lookup
2
Optimize the route
Shortest drive time calculated using real traffic data for your session date. Drag to reorder or adjust times manually.
🗺️ Live traffic routing
3
Request showings via ShowingTime
Pre-formatted ShowingTime request blocks per property. Status updates automatically via API Nation + GHL webhook.
🔄 Auto status sync
4
Calendar blocks created instantly
Tentative showing events and travel buffers added to Google Calendar. Confirmed = updated. Declined = deleted.
📅 Google Calendar
5
Property research on confirmation
Zillow data pulled automatically when a showing confirms. Price history, DOM, school district — ready before you walk in.
🏠 Auto research
6
Disclosure PDF analysis
Upload the disclosure, get a red-flag report in seconds. Severity ratings with exact seller disclosure language quoted.
🔴 Red flag detection
7
Build the client itinerary page
One-click generation of a branded client-facing page with photos, times, addresses, and a Google Calendar invite link.
📤 Client delivery
8
Session complete
Zero tentative calendar events remain. Client page delivered. Session archived. Summary: confirmed, declined, pending counts.
✅ Full audit trail
